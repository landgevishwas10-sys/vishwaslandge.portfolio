How I Work

The Strategy Behind
Every Brand

01

Discovery & Brand Audit

I start by deeply understanding your business, target audience, and competitors. Every strategy is built on real data — not templates. This phase includes a full audit of your existing social presence, tone, and content gaps.

02

Strategy & Content Blueprint

I develop a custom content strategy with content pillars, posting cadence, platform recommendations, and a monthly calendar. You'll know exactly what's being posted, why, and what business outcome it's driving.

03

Create, Schedule & Publish

Content is created — copy, direction, creative briefs — scheduled in advance and published at peak performance times. I handle everything from captions to hashtag strategy to reel structuring.

04

Engage & Manage Community

I monitor comments, respond to DMs, and keep your community active and warm. Engagement isn't optional — it's a growth lever I treat seriously on every account I manage.

05

Analyze, Report & Optimize

Monthly performance reports with clear metrics, honest insights, and actionable next steps. I adjust the strategy based on what data tells us — and I never stop iterating.
